# Custom domain: Setup guide

To connect a custom domain to your Base44 app, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your domain" titleSize="h3">
    - Use only lowercase letters. Remove any trailing spaces or tabs.

      <Note>
        Domains with uppercase letters or extra whitespace won't verify correctly.
      </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the domain" titleSize="h3">
    - Go to **Workspace → Domains**.
    - Click **Add Domain** and enter your domain (e.g., [mycoolapp.com](http://mycoolapp.com)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="DNS configuration" titleSize="h3">
    Set the following at your domain registrar:

    - **ANAME / ALIAS Record** _(if supported)_
      - **Name**: @
    - **Value**: [base44.onrender.com](http://base44.onrender.com)
    - **If ANAME isn't supported, use A Record instead**
      - **Name**: @
      - **Value**: 216.24.57.1\
        ❗ Make sure this is the **only A record**
    - **CNAME Record (www)**
      - **Name**: www
      - **Value**: [base44.onrender.com](http://base44.onrender.com)
    - _Optional:_ **To connect a subdomain** (e.g., [good.example.com](http://good.example.com))
      - **Name**: subdomain prefix (e.g., good)
      - **Value**: [base44.onrender.com](http://base44.onrender.com)

    ❌ Remove any **AAAA records**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the domain" titleSize="h3">
    - Go to **Workspace → Domains**.
    - Click **Verify** next to your domain. 

      <Tip>
        DNS changes may take up to 48 hours.
      </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reusing domains from deleted apps" titleSize="h3">
    If the domain was used in an app deleted **before June 5, 2025**, contact Support to clear it from Base44's records before reuse.
  </Step>
</Steps>
